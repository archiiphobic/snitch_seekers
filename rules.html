How to Play Quidditch

Welcome to Snitch Seekers! New to the game? You should know the following things about Quidditch:

  • Each team has 7 players — 3 Chasers, 2 Beaters, 1 Keeper, and 1 Seeker.
  • The goal of the game is to score more points than the opposing team by throwing the Quaffle through one of the opponent’s three goal hoops.
  • Chasers score goals with the Quaffle, earning 10 points per goal.
  • Beaters use bats to hit the Bludgers toward opponents to knock them off their brooms or disrupt their play.
  • The Seeker's job is to catch the Golden Snitch, which is worth 150 points.
  • The Keeper guards the goal hoops and tries to block the Quaffle from going through.
  • Catching the Snitch ends the game and earns the seeker’s team 150 points, often deciding the winner.

Enjoy playing Snitch Seekers!

GAME RULES

  • Rule 1: Respect other players and their choices.
  • Rule 2: Follow the game's objectives and guidelines.
  • Rule 3: Each team has assigned roles (Scorer, Defender, Disruptor, Seeker), and players must play within their role’s abilities.
  • Rule 4: No cheating or exploiting game mechanics.
  • Rule 5: Score points by sending the main ball through the opponent’s goal zones.
  • Rule 6: Avoid or block disruptor objects that can temporarily slow, stun, or knock players off course.
  • Rule 7: A special target (like a Golden Snitch) appears randomly, and capturing it gives a large point bonus and may end the match.
  • Rule 8: Fair play is required — no cheating, exploiting bugs, or using offensive language; violations may result in penalties or bans.
  • Rule 9: Report any bugs or issues to the game moderators.
  • Rule 10: Have fun and enjoy the game!
